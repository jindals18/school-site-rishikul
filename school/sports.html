SPORTS

Rishikul Vidyapeeth offers a variety of sporting activities aimed to improve the health and fitness of the students and nurture the individual talents whilst enabling them to enjoy the recreational benefits. School enables students to have team skills through competitive sports that inculcate a sense of sportsmanship such as trust, commitment, self-discipline, honesty, respect for the opponent, whether you win or lose. Students inevitably leave Rishikul with a heightened appreciation for the outdoors and an understanding of what it takes to succeed in challenging environment. And this is possible as school campuses have superb indoor & outdoor sporting facilities which provide a wide range of competitive development and recreational sports to be offered throughout the year. We offer a pathway for sporting development from kindergarten to grade 12.
